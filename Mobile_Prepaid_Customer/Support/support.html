What is the ₹319 plan validity?

+

Step 1: This plan is valid for a calendar month.

Step 2: If you recharge on 5th March, next recharge is due on 5th April.

Step 3: Enjoy postpaid-like convenience with prepaid.

How can I make a prepaid recharge?

+

Step 1: Select a suitable prepaid recharge.

Step 2: Click "Buy" and proceed to payment.

Step 3: Choose Credit/Debit card or Net Banking.

What is the validity cycle for my plan?

+

Step 1: The plan remains valid for a calendar month.

Step 2: Recharge on 5th March? Next due date is 5th April.

How can I recharge my mobile online?

+

Step 1: Go to the Vi webpage or Vi app.

Step 2: Select your mobile number & recharge amount.

Step 3: Choose payment method & confirm.

How do I check my plan details?

+

Step 1: Dial *199# on your mobile.

Step 2: Open your telecom provider's app.

Step 3: Navigate to "My Plan" section.
